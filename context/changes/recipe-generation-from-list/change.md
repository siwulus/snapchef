Below the list of recognized and corrected ingredients, there should be the textarea for the free text written by the customer. Customer can write anything about what he would like to have in this recipe. If the recipe should be limited to only recognized products, or can also be composed with others which are not on the list, etc. It would be valuable to give such a hint as a description of this textarea to make the customer conscious of his influence the receip.

After providing this additional information, the edited recognized items plus additional description is sent to the server, and the final recipe is generated.

Generated recipe is saved on a server before returning. It doesn't mean that we have finihed the whole session as saved and done. The final decision is still waiting, and it's the matter of the next use case.

recipe contains the content and name. Name is also generated by AI.
